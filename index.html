  • Sorting:
    • Click on the arrow next to a column name. You can sort in ascending or descending order.
    • To sort multiple columns, hold shift and then click on the arrow next to the columns' names.
  • Filtering:
    • Provide a search phrase within the search box to filter the data
    • For example, if you want to filter out only drugs related to CYB5R gene, use "CYB5R" keyword in the search box
  • Viewing:
    • Depending on your screen size, some columns might get hidden automatically. To access the hidden columns click on the plus sign shown at the beginning of each row.
  • Exporting:
    • To export filtered or sorted data, click on the export to CSV file
